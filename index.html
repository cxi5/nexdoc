How to use
1
Tap the + button to create a new contract. Fill in the title, type, parties and value.
2
Write content in the text editor — you have Word-like formatting tools.
3
Save as Draft or send to Sign. Documents are stored in Archive.
4
Open a pending contract and draw your digital signature directly on screen.
5
Tap Print / PDF — the dialog opens automatically, choose "Save as PDF".
Features
✦
Rich Editor — bold, italic, headings, lists, tables and colours.
✦
Digital Signature with SHA-256 hash for authenticity.
✦
Import files TXT or DOC to edit directly.
✦
Offline data — everything saved on your device, no server.
✦
Export PDF — choose "Save as PDF" in the print dialog.
